# Prioritize Visual Spectacle and Wish Fidelity

When core goals conflict, Infinite Starwish prioritizes visual spectacle first, then LLM creative latitude and wish fidelity, then playable performance, then numerical balance, then implementation speed. The game's differentiator is the freshness of custom visual expression from wishes, so engineering choices should protect that fantasy before optimizing for conventional balance or minimal scope.

Visual acceptance may use official Vampire Survivors screenshots stored under `docs/visual-reference/vampire-survivors/` to calibrate polish, horde density, readability, projectile saturation, and late-run energy. Those screenshots are not style references or asset sources; Infinite Starwish remains Neon Pixel Space and Code-Drawn Art.
